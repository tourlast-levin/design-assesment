# **Tourlast Product Design Assessment**

Hello! We’re excited to see your creativity in action.

As part of this interview, we’d like you to design **at least TWO (2)** of the following for the **Tourlast** brand:

* Flier
* Business Card
* Event Backdrop
* Tear Drop Banner
* T-Shirt Mockup
* Notebook
* Mug Mockup
* Any Tourlast Merch Item of your choice

You do **not need to design all items**. We care about **quality over quantity**. Choose the items you feel will best showcase your design abilities and alignment with our brand.

## **Objective**

At Tourlast, we help people **explore the world with ease and confidence**. Our brand is about **adventure, convenience, and trust**, delivered in a **modern, professional, and approachable style**.

For this task, we want to see how you bring that to life in a tangible product. Your designs should:

* Feel like Tourlast, energetic but trustworthy
* Inspire exploration and curiosity
* Look professional while being friendly and inviting
* Work in the real world, not just on a screen

We’re evaluating **not just how pretty it looks**, but how your design communicates **our values and personality**.

## **Deliverables**

Please submit the following for your selected items:

1. **Final Mockup** – a realistic, presentation-ready view (how it would look in the real world)
2. **Print-Ready Export** – correct dimensions, bleed included if needed, high-resolution files suitable for production
3. **Editable Source File** – Figma, Illustrator, or PSD
4. **Short Explanation** (1 page max) – describing:

   * Your design concept and reasoning
   * How your design fits the Tourlast brand
   * How it could scale to other brand items (e.g., a flier → t-shirt → backdrop)

## **What We’re Looking For**

We want to see **you think like a Tourlast designer**. That means:

* **Consistency with our brand** – colors, typography, mood, and tone
* **Print & merch readiness** – designs that are practical for production
* **Scalable thinking** – could this design translate to other items or formats?
* **Professional presentation** – clarity, polish, and attention to detail
* **Creativity & originality** – fresh ideas that feel human, not generic

Remember, this is your chance to show **how you bring a brand to life in the real world**, not just on a screen.

## **Human Touch / Inspiration**

Think about our users: travelers who are **curious, excited, and seeking ease**. Your designs should make them feel **welcome, inspired, and confident**.

* For a flier or backdrop, imagine it’s the first thing a traveler sees at a tourism fair.
* For a tee or mug, imagine it’s something someone proudly carries on their adventures.
* For a business card, imagine it’s leaving a lasting impression with partners and clients.

We want **every element to tell our story**: Tourlast is about exploration, simplicity, and trust, make it **feel alive** in your design.

## **Judging Criteria**

| Criteria                   | Weight |
| -------------------------- | ------ |
| Brand Fit                  | High   |
| Creativity                 | High   |
| Technical Execution        | High   |
| Practicality & Scalability | High   |
| Presentation & Detail      | Medium |

We are not looking for perfection in every pixel, but **thoughtfulness, clarity, and alignment with our brand voice**.
